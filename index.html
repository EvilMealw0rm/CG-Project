Computer Graphics Project

Animated movie - A boat trip

A computer graphics project by Edith Karda and Christopher Rumplmayr

This repository contains the CG-Project for the course Computer Graphics at the Johannes Kepler University Linz.

The movie will automatically end after 30 seconds and then the autopilot ends.
To toggle manual camera control and autopilot, press M on the keyboard.
W - move forward
A - strafe left
S - move backwards
D - strafe right

R can be used to reset the camera
